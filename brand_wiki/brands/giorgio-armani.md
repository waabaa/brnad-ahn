## 시작과 성장

이탈리아 럭셔리 패션 브랜드인 조르지오 아르마니는 1975년에 설립되었다. 창립자인 조르지오 아르마니는 본래 의학도였다. 그는 백화점 머천다이저로 일하면서 패션계에 입문했으며, 실용적이고 고급스러운 재킷을 디자인하여 '재킷의 왕'이라는 칭송을 얻었다. 오늘날 브랜드 조르지오 아르마니는 패션은 물론 호텔 및 가전사업에도 진출하여 토탈 브랜드로 자리매김하고 있다.

## 확장 지식

Italian fashion designer (1934–2025) Giorgio Armani was an Italian fashion designer and founder of the Armani luxury fashion house. Widely regarded as among the most influential designers in contemporary fashion, Armani initially gained recognition for his work with fashion house Cerruti 1881, before founding his own label in 1975. He became known for minimalist, deconstructed silhouettes—especially his jackets and suits—which are said to have redefined masculine and feminine elegance in a contemporary form. Armani also played a pivotal role in shaping celebrity style, particularly red-carpet fashion. By the early 2000s, he was recognized as the most successful Italian designer, with his brand expanding into music, sport, and luxury hotels.
